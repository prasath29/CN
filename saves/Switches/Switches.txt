Difference between HUB and SWITCH

Switch is a networking hardware that connects devices on a computer network to establish a local area network.stores MAC address table.It works on the layer 2.It do unicasting,multicasting and broadcasting.


Hub is a networking hardware that connects devices on a computer network to establish a local area network.It works on layer 1.
 
Hub has no memory.But,Switch has memory
